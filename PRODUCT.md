# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors are recruiters, professors, collaborators, and peers who need a fast, credible read on Xiling Wang's academic background, data science experience, research work, and selected projects.

## Product Purpose

This site is a personal portfolio and academic profile. It should make the career story legible, separate dense resume sections into navigable pages, and help visitors find research, experience, projects, teaching, skills, and contact details without scrolling through one long document.

## Brand Personality

Rigorous, analytical, grounded. The tone should feel like a well-edited research dossier: confident, factual, and readable rather than decorative or template-like.

## Anti-references

Avoid one-page resume sprawl, oversized rounded glass cards, empty gradient backgrounds, repeated card grids, generic tech-portfolio styling, and tiny uppercase section labels repeated as a crutch.

## Design Principles

- Split content by visitor intent so each page has one clear job.
- Make evidence easy to scan: roles, dates, institutions, methods, datasets, and outcomes should be visible without digging.
- Use restraint with structure: grids, rules, and typographic hierarchy should carry the page instead of large decorative containers.
- Preserve credibility over flash; motion and color should clarify navigation and hierarchy.
- Keep the site static and GitHub Pages friendly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible navigation, responsive layouts for mobile and desktop, and reduced-motion fallbacks for transitions.
